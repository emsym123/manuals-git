setenv bootcmd ‘nand read ${loadaddr} 0x1000000 0x400000;bootm’ 烧写 setenv bootargs ‘console=ttymxc3,115200 rdinit=/linuxrc mtdparts=gpmi-nand:16m(boot),16m(kernel),420m(rootfs),-(user)arm_freq=800’
